What's New — v2.3 (21)
(covers everything since the last LIVE version, v2.1 — v2.2 was never released)

Two ways to keep your documents in order:

• Merge documents — got a document split across two scans? Touch and hold one, choose Merge into…, and its pages join another, text and all.

• Name your signatures — give each saved signature a name so you can tell them apart when it's time to sign.
