Banana Bread

Grandma's classic — moist, simple, foolproof
Prep 15 min · Bake 60 min · Makes 1 loaf

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Butter a 4x8-inch loaf pan.
  2. Mix mashed bananas with the melted butter.
  3. Stir in sugar, egg, and vanilla.
  4. Sprinkle the baking soda and salt over the mixture and stir in.
  5. Add the flour and mix until just combined.
  6. Pour batter into the pan. Bake 50–60 minutes.
  7. Cool on a rack before slicing.
