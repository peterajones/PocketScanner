Consulting Services Agreement

Agreement No. MAG-2026-0417  ·  Confidential

This Consulting Services Agreement (the “Agreement”) is entered into by and between Meridian Advisory Group, LLC, a California limited liability company (the “Company”), and Jordan Avery, an independent contractor (the “Consultant”), effective as of the date of last signature below.

1. Engagement & Services

The Company retains the Consultant to provide strategic advisory and operational review services (the “Services”) as described in one or more statements of work. The Consultant shall perform the Services in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with prevailing industry standards.

2. Term

This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for a period of twelve (12) months, unless earlier terminated in accordance with Section 6. Either party may renew for successive terms upon written agreement.

3. Compensation

The Company shall pay the Consultant a fee of $185.00 per hour, invoiced monthly and payable within thirty (30) days of receipt. The Company shall reimburse reasonable, pre-approved expenses incurred in the performance of the Services.

4. Independent Contractor

The Consultant is an independent contractor and not an employee, partner, or agent of the Company. The Consultant is responsible for all taxes arising from compensation paid hereunder.

5. Confidentiality

Each party shall hold in confidence all non-public information disclosed by the other and shall use such information solely to perform its obligations under this Agreement. This obligation survives termination for a period of three (3) years.

6. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement upon fifteen (15) days’ written notice. Upon termination, the Consultant shall be paid for Services properly rendered through the effective date of termination.
